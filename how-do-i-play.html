Othello

How do I play?

You take turns placing stones on the squares of the board. You may only play stones that capture some of the other player's stones. Any stones in between yours and the other player's are captured, and flip over to show the opposite color. For example:

The square on the right is not a valid move, because it doesn't border any white stones and would not capture anything. If black plays on the left, both white stones would be captured and become black.

If you cannot capture any of the other player's stones, you must pass. The app will determine this for you and give control back to the other player after showing "PASS" on your part of the score board at the top. For example:

When the board is full, the game is over. Whichever player has the most stones wins. The app will show "WIN" on the score board at the top. For example:

You can start the game over at any time using the "Reset Game" button at the bottom.

Playing locally

To play locally, you can use a touch screen, track pad, mouse or any other pointing device. Players take turns clicking or tapping on the same device to make their moves. This works really well with a tablet laying on the table between two players.

Playing offline

To play offline, just load the URL. If you've loaded it before, it should be cached and available offline. On some platforms, such as Android, you may also be prompted to install the app to your home screen.

Playing with a friend over the internet

To play over the internet, click or tap on the "P2P Multi-player" button at the bottom of the screen. A video chat will appear to the right of or beneath the game board. Click or tap on the text under "Your ID" to copy it to the clipboard. Send this text to a friend. Your friend then pastes your ID into the field labelled "Your friend's ID" and hits enter on the keyboard or tablet/phone's on-screen keyboard. This will start the connection between you and your friend.

During the game, you can see and talk to your friend while you play. There is a mute button which can be used to mute or unmute the connection. When either end is muted, neither of you can hear each other. You can use the "X" button on the video to terminate the connection and go back to local play.

When it's your turn, your color will have a halo behind it at the top of the scoreboard, and all valid moves will be marked for you with a gray dot. Either player can use the "Reset Game" button at the bottom of the board at any time to start a new game.

This game is meant to be social and played with friends and family. It is not meant as a way to meet internet trolls, so there is no match-making service. You'll have to send your randomly-generated ID to others using some other system, like a text message, chat, or email.

Browser support

This probably won't work on IE. It should work on any current browser. If it doesn't work for you, please file a new issue here: https://github.com/cashpipeplusplus/othello/issues
